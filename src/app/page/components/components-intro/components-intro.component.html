Intro

The best way to get familiar with a library or framework is to build a component library.

It doesn't matter about which area is the topic: front-end, back-end, etc.

This section is committed to the UI.

Here I will show how to build components with Angular and Bootstrap.

Of course, one may ask: "What about ready to use solutions?".

And my answer is: "Go ahead and use them!".

Such solutions saving a lot of time for developers and are easy to use.

But, if you want to deep dive into the ecosystem, you should invest your time and patience.

That's why I'm offering you to build several components.
